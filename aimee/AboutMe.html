About Me

Designer

Hi there! My name is Aimee Zhao. I'm pursuing a major in Design and a minor in Computer Science. I have also had the opportunity to explore a variety of design mediums, including graphic design, web design, and product design.

In addition to my studies in Design, I have a keen interest in Computer Science. I have taken several courses in programming languages and have experience working with data structures, algorithms, and software development methodologies. I believe that my knowledge in both design and computer science can complement each other and enable me to create unique and innovative solutions to complex problems.

I am a creative problem solver who enjoys challenges and has a passion for technology. I'm constantly looking for new opportunities to learn and grow, and I'm excited to see where my education and experience will take me in the future. Thank you for taking the time to get to know me, and I look forward to sharing more with you.
